Short, no-bounce. Atlas is calm and considered — no spring physics, no 600ms reveals. Cosmetic motion is disabled under prefers-reduced-motion; map pan/zoom remains functional.

07

Voice

Write like you're explaining a place to a friend who's about to buy a plane ticket. No breathless marketing copy, no apocalyptic weather talk. Observations before adjectives.

Do

atlas · observed
"Lima rarely sees rain. It also rarely sees the sun — expect a cool grey quilt for most of April."
"13–19°C, all month, almost every year."
"Perfect for long walks. Poor for a beach holiday."

Don't

drift · avoid
"Unlock Lima's perfect weather today!"
"Incredible 13°C temperatures await 🌴"
"Absolutely stunning climate conditions."
